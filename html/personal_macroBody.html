Skin Deep

Macrophotography and Video Portraits

Spring, 2019 | Category: Photography and Video | Canon 6D MarkII

This project started with my fun exploration of reversing my 24-135mm lens, which made ordinary things sensational, like under a microscope. Unexpectedly, the personal marcrophotography turned into my Senior Show video portraits. The installation made the 2D videos turned into a 3D sculpture. I purposefully only used a corner of the room, surrounded the monitors with cheesecloth, and set up a bench in front of the monitors to make the viewers feel the intimacy.
