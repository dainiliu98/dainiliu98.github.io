Japan Countryside

Spring, 2019 | Category: Digital Illustration | iPad Procreate

This is my personal fun project, one of the very first illustrations I did on Procreate. It is based on several photos I took near my school in Chiba, Japan, which is a very significant place for me, as I had my best semester studying abroad and shared many good memories with my close friends.

I am planning on doing more landscape illustrations in this style: geometric shapes, earth tone colors, and grainy texture. I am trying to make them more abstract and bold. Less is more.
